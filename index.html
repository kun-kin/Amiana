Amiana - An Impeccable Luxury Destination

Overlooking the sparkling waters of the stunning Nha Trang Bay, Amiana is a five-star, award-winning resort featuring 153 spacious villas, each with garden or ocean views and a private balcony. At the heart of our resort is a stunning 2,500 sqm natural seawater pool, a magnificent 700 sqm freshwater infinity pool, and a private, guest-only lagoon beach with soft, white sand leading into crystal clear waters. Luxury is built-in to every aspect of the resort.

Accommodation

We offer a truly private and unique atmosphere for individuals, couples or families who seek an escape from everyday life. Ranging from 55sqm to 100sqm for Amiana Deluxe Collection and from 65 sqm to over 450 sqm for Amiana Villas Collection, our rooms and villas echo and embrace the local touch, be it a Bat Trang tea set, coffee filter, construction materials made from local coconut products, bamboo or natural stone gathered from nearby.

Deluxe Room Mountain View With Children Themed Room

The ultimate in luxury, our Pool Villas 3-bedroom Ocean View are perfectly designed for family holidays or groups of friends. Each Pool Villa 3-bedroom Ocean View offers plenty of room to relax within 450 sqm, including 2 King size bedrooms & 1 Twin bedroom (maximum 6 adults & 2 children), a spacious living and dining room, and a fully-equipped kitchen.
