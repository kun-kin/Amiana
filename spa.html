Spa & Mudbath

Nha Trang has long been famed for its natural mud bath and herbal treatments. It’s now a lot more famous as we introduce the one and only luxury, private mud bath with ocean view in Nha Trang. Together with a wide range of sensual, soothing and rejuvenating treatments, each drawing from the traditions of Asia and the sciences of Europe, makes a visit to our Spa a must during your stay at Amiana.

  • Total 1,040 sqm in area
  • Reception/Waiting area with garden view
  • Relaxing lounge with ocean view
  • 2 VIP treatment rooms – Lotus/Orchid
  • 2 couple treatment rooms – Lavender/Jasmine
  • 4 single treatment rooms – Rose/Citrus/Sandalwood/Ylang-Ylang
  • 1 Thai room – Tea Tree
  • Beauty salon
  • Separate lockers for men and women
  • Separate steam, sauna, and Jacuzzi pools for men and women
